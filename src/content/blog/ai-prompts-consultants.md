# AI Prompts for Consultants: Deliver More, Write Less

Consulting is fundamentally about insight and communication. Clients pay you for your judgment — but they experience that judgment through your deliverables, proposals, reports, and presentations.

AI can't replace your expertise. But it can dramatically reduce the time between thinking it and writing it.

## What Can AI Help Consultants With?

- Project proposals and statement-of-work documents
- Client-ready reports and executive summaries
- Slide deck outlines and speaker notes
- Workshop and meeting facilitation guides
- Post-engagement follow-up communications
- Marketing: case studies, LinkedIn, thought leadership articles
- Internal templates and IP documentation
- Research summarization

## The Best AI Prompts for Consultants

### How do I write a consulting proposal with AI?

**Prompt:**
> "Write a consulting proposal for the following engagement: Client: [DESCRIPTION]. Problem they're trying to solve: [DESCRIPTION]. My proposed approach: [3-5 PHASES OR COMPONENTS]. Deliverables: [LIST]. Timeline: [DURATION]. Fees: [STRUCTURE — I'll add specifics]. Include: situation summary, proposed scope, approach, timeline overview, and investment section headers."

A solid proposal draft in 5 minutes. You add the strategy; AI builds the structure.

### How do I write an executive summary for a consulting report?

**Prompt:**
> "Write an executive summary for a consulting report on the following engagement: Client background: [BRIEF]. Project objective: [WHAT YOU WERE HIRED TO DO]. Key findings: [3-5 BULLET POINTS]. Recommendations: [LIST]. Format: 1 page maximum. Audience: C-suite. Lead with the most important recommendation, not the background."

### How do I create a workshop agenda with AI?

**Prompt:**
> "Design a half-day workshop agenda for [CLIENT TYPE] focused on [TOPIC]. Goals: [LIST 2-3 OUTCOMES]. Attendees: [ROLES / NUMBER]. Include: welcome, icebreaker, agenda overview, 2-3 working sessions with exercises, lunch break, synthesis/decision session, and close. Add time blocks and brief facilitator notes for each section."

### How can AI help me write client presentation speaker notes?

**Prompt:**
> "Write speaker notes for a consulting presentation slide deck with the following slides: [LIST SLIDE TITLES]. Each slide: 3-5 sentences of talking points. Keep it conversational — these are for speaking, not reading. Anticipate one likely question per slide and note how to handle it."

### How do I write a case study for my consulting firm?

**Prompt:**
> "Write a client case study for my consulting firm. Structure: Challenge / Approach / Results. Client details: [INDUSTRY, SIZE — anonymized if needed]. Challenge they faced: [DESCRIPTION]. My approach: [WHAT YOU DID]. Results achieved: [METRICS OR QUALITATIVE OUTCOMES]. Length: 400-500 words. Tone: confident, outcome-focused, not self-congratulatory."

### How do I write a post-engagement summary email?

**Prompt:**
> "Write a post-engagement email to a client after completing [PROJECT TYPE]. Summarize: what was accomplished, key decisions made, next steps they own, and how I can continue to support them. Warm but professional. Include a soft door-opener for future work."

## More Prompts for Your Practice

**For thought leadership articles:**
> "Write a 600-word thought leadership article for LinkedIn on [TOPIC RELEVANT TO YOUR EXPERTISE]. Audience: [TARGET CLIENT PROFILE]. Structure: provocative opening, 3 key insights with examples, clear takeaway. Tone: authoritative but accessible. No buzzwords."

**For SOW documents:**
> "Write a Statement of Work for [PROJECT TYPE]. Include: project overview, objectives, scope inclusions, scope exclusions, deliverables, assumptions, timeline, payment terms. Format as a formal document. I'll add the specific numbers."

**For client discovery prep:**
> "Help me prepare for a discovery call with a potential client. Their situation: [WHAT YOU KNOW]. What I need to understand: [YOUR GOALS FOR THE CALL]. Generate 12 open-ended discovery questions organized by: current state, desired state, constraints, decision process, and stakeholders."

**For pricing conversations:**
> "Write a short email response to a client who has asked about my fees for [PROJECT TYPE]. My range for this type of work is [RANGE]. I want to explain value before quoting a number. Keep it under 150 words. Confident, not defensive."

## Why Consultants Who Use AI Win More Work

The consultants using AI aren't cutting corners — they're compressing timelines. A proposal that used to take 4 hours takes 45 minutes. That frees capacity for more client conversations, deeper thinking, and better work.

Clients can't tell if a proposal was drafted with AI assistance. They can tell if it's clear, well-structured, and directly addresses their problem. That's all AI does — it helps you get to the good draft faster.
